## Understanding Compensation

Before you start calculating payroll, it's important to understand how staff get paid.

### 1. Compensation Profiles (Pay Structures)

Each staff member chooses one of these options:

| Profile Name | Hourly Rate | Commission Rate | Best For |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| **High Base, Low Commission** | $25/hour | 5% | New staff, prefer stability |
| **Balanced** *(Default)* | $18/hour | 15% | Most staff, good mix |
| **Commission Heavy** | $12/hour | 30% | Experienced, high performers |
| **Pure Commission** | $0/hour | 50% | Top performers, self-motivated |

**Example:**
- Sarah chooses "Balanced" ($18/hr + 15% commission)
- She works 40 hours and earns $4,000 in service revenue
- Her pay = ($18 Ã— 40) + ($4,000 Ã— 15%) = $720 + $600 = **$1,320**

### 2. Performance Tiers

Each month, staff are automatically placed in a tier based on completed appointments:

| Tier | Appointments | Commission Boost | Monthly Bonus |
|------|--------------|------------------|---------------|
| ðŸ¥‰ **Bronze** | 0-20 | Normal (1x) | $0 |
| ðŸ¥ˆ **Silver** | 21-40 | +20% (1.2x) | $100 |
| ðŸ¥‡ **Gold** | 41-60 | +50% (1.5x) | $300 |
| ðŸ’Ž **Platinum** | 61+ | Double (2x) | $500 |

**Example:**

- Her commission gets boosted by 50%
- Base commission: $600 â†’ Gold commission: $600 Ã— 1.5 = **$900**
- Plus she gets a **$300 bonus** for reaching Gold!

### 3. Bonus Types

You can give bonuses for many reasons:

- ðŸŽ **Custom**: Any reason (e.g., "Great job this month!")

### 4. How Hours are Calculated

**Important**: You don't need to track attendance!

The system automatically estimates hours based on:

### Step-by-Step Workflow

## Common Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: How often should I calculate payroll?**
A: Once per month, at the beginning of the new month for the previous month.

**Q: Can I recalculate payroll?**
A: Yes! You can recalculate as many times as needed before approving.

**Q: What if a staff member has no appointments?**
A: They'll show $0 commission, but may still have base pay if their profile includes hourly rate.

**Q: Can I delete a payroll record?**
A: No, but you can add correction bonuses (positive or negative amounts).

### Calculation Questions

**Q: How are hours calculated without a time clock?**
A: Hours are estimated from appointment service durations + 15 min buffer per appointment.

**Q: Why is Sarah's commission different from last month?**
A: Commission depends on: service revenue, commission rate, and performance tier. Any of these can change monthly.

**Q: What if I forgot to add a bonus?**
A: Add the bonus, then recalculate payroll. If already paid, add it to next month.

**Q: Can bonuses be negative?**
A: Yes! Use negative amounts for deductions (e.g., "-50" for uniform fee).

### Performance Tier Questions

**Q: How is tier determined?**
A: Based on completed appointments count for that month only.

**Q: Can someone be Platinum one month and Bronze the next?**
A: Yes! Tiers reset each month based on that month's performance.

**Q: Do tier bonuses carry over?**
A: No, tier bonuses are earned fresh each month.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Does the system send payments automatically?**
A: No, you must process actual payments separately. The system only calculates amounts.

**Q: Can I pay staff early?**
A: Yes, but calculate payroll first to know the amounts.

**Q: What if I overpaid someone?**
A: Deduct the overpayment in next month's payroll using a negative bonus.

### System Questions

**Q: Can staff see their own payroll?**
A: Currently, only admins can see payroll. (Future enhancement: staff portal)

**Q: Can I customize the compensation profiles?**
A: Advanced users can add new profiles via the database. Contact support for help.

**Q: Can I change someone's pay structure mid-month?**
A: Not recommended. Changes take effect next month when you recalculate.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: The web interface is mobile-responsive, but no native app yet.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "No payroll data for this period"

**Cause**: Payroll hasn't been calculated yet for the selected month.

**Solution**: Click "ðŸ”„ Calculate Payroll" button.

---

### Issue: Hours showing 0 for a staff member

**Possible Causes**:
1. No completed appointments for that month
2. Appointments missing service duration data

**Solution**:
1. Check appointments table: Do they have completed appointments?
2. Check services: Do services have duration values set?
3. If services lack durations, add them in Services page

---

### Issue: Commission is $0

**Possible Causes**:
1. Staff has 0% commission rate in their profile
2. No service revenue for the month
3. All appointments are cancelled/pending

**Solution**:
1. Check staff compensation profile (should have commission_rate > 0)
2. Verify appointments status = "completed"
3. Check appointment_services table has services with prices

---

### Issue: Bonuses not showing in payroll

**Cause**: Forgot to recalculate after adding bonus.

**Solution**: Click "ðŸ”„ Calculate Payroll" again after adding bonuses.

---

### Issue: Can't approve payroll (button missing)

**Possible Causes**:
1. Status is "draft" (not calculated yet)
2. Already approved (shows "ðŸ’³ Mark Paid" instead)
3. Already paid (no buttons)

**Solution**: Check the status badge color to see current state.

---

### Issue: Export CSV is empty

**Cause**: No earnings data for selected filters.

**Solution**:
1. Change year filter
2. Select "All Staff" instead of individual
3. Verify payroll has been calculated and approved for some months

---

### Issue: Numbers don't add up

**Example**: Base pay + commission + bonuses â‰  net pay

**Possible Causes**:
1. Deductions (rare, usually $0)
2. Rounding differences
3. Calculation bug

**Solution**:
1. Check if deductions > 0
2. Recalculate payroll (may fix rounding)
3. If still wrong, contact support with details

---

### Issue: System is slow

**Possible Causes**:
1. Calculating very large payroll (50+ staff)
2. Many appointments in the period
3. Internet connection issues

**Solution**:
1. Wait patiently (up to 10 seconds for large salons)
2. Check internet connection
3. Refresh page if stuck >30 seconds
